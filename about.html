Why

Parents want to be able to keep track of their childrens progress in school.

Which is why we have created the Child Progress Tracker

Now you can do all that at the touch of a button.


Child Assessment

The Child Progress Tracker provides a picture of the child's progress and identifies any problem areas that require further support.

Parent will be able to understand how well their children are faring in school



Parent Communication

The teacher is your partner in educating your child

This platform bridges the communication gap between the parents and the teachers and makes information about the child accessible to parents on the go.



Teacher Documentation

CHILD PROGRESS TRACKER provide teachers with the necessary tools for Assessment and Evaluation

It also serves as a frame work for feedback to acertain Areas that need improvement

Overall CPT saves time, space and money spent on paper and is easily accessible for future reference
